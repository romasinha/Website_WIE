About Us

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering.
The IEEE WIE MUJ Chapter aspires to follow through this vision of the parent organisation and has made a lot of progress since its conception on our campus in 2019. It has grown to become a community where women in technology interact and make big things happen!

Events

We at IEEE WIE MUJ organise various technical and non-technical events that help young girls and women to enhance their overall personality as well as help them prepare them to be competent in the global technical deciplines. Our events have been well recieved by the audiences of MUJ students and other universities too.

Blogs

IEEE WIE MUJ has a Medium publication which promotes technical and non technical writing amongst women. The team helps and provides input to all interested and aspiring bloggers who have something to say about the world around them. The club's working team uploads blogs written by our members on various topics every second week on the IEEE WIE MUJ medium publication.

Follow us on medium to read our blogs.

Interactions

We at IEEE WIE MUJ believe in strong interaction within our community. There are frequent interactive meetings scheduled every month, where all the club members come together and attend various brain stroming activities and personality devlopement programs apart from the technical workshps. These interactive sessions are a safe and comfertable space where the members can come out their ideas and suggestions.
